What I do

I have a few different interests.

IT and Software Development

Liking computers and technology since a very young age, I started using and expirimenting with linux, and then programming, when I was only 13. Now, I have been an admin for my various servers for over 3 years, created more than a few websites, and have developed a variety of software including the control code for a robot in the 2016 FIRST Competition.

Film and Photography

I not only enjoy watching good films and taking in creative pictures, but I enjoy making them too. Since the start of my school's video course, I have had a great time creating a variety of different short videos. For a recent project, I incorporated photography with video, using pictures of the daytime clouds, and long exposures of night streets and stars, to create a short time-lapse movie. I work mostly in proffesional software such as Photoshop and Premiere.

Electrical Engineering and Mechanics

I have been a tinkerer for a very long time. I started out making my own flashlights from scavenged parts in kindergarten, to repairing and building computers, phones, appliances, and robots. I have experience designing and creating custom circuits with integrated microprocessors as a hobby. Repairing bicycles, engines, and other machines are also activites I enjoy.
